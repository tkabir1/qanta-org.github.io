# Prizes and Awards

QANTA 2026 includes prizes for participants across tracks, including computer systems, human teams, and question writing.

## Human Team Prizes

Top placements in the live human competition receive:

- **1st place**: $250
- **2nd place**: $150
- **3rd place**: $50

## Computer Team Prizes

Computer systems are ranked using size-adjusted performance as
described in the [computer rules](/competition/2026/rules/computer/).

- **Top Computer Team per Class**: $200
- **Second Place Computer Team per Class**: $100

## Writing Prizes

- **Best question (quality)**: $50
- **Best question (adversarial)**: $50
- **Best packet (quality)**: $100
- **Best packet (adversarial)**: $100

Packet winners are ineligible for question-prize payouts in the same category, though they may still receive recognition.

## Question Writing Payments

Question authors receive:

- **$5 per accepted question**

See the author instructions for submission details and acceptance criteria:

> [For Authors](/competition/2026/authors/)

## Prize Eligibility and Payments

All prize winners must be eligible to receive funds through the Tango payout platform:

> [Tango](https://www.tangocard.com/)

## Workshop Recognition

Winning systems and notable shared-task results will be highlighted in connection with the ICML 2026 EMM-QA workshop.

> [EMM-QA Workshop Page](/competition/2026/icml/)
